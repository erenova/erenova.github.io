About Me!

Hello! I'm Eren a 17-year-old web development enthusiast. My journey began two years ago with FreeCodeCamp, where I obtained two certificates that cemented my understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With this foundational knowledge, I independently mastered Bootstrap, a skill showcased through the design and functionality of this very website. In August 2022, after concluding my journey with FreeCodeCamp, I embarked on a new educational endeavor with The Odin Project the following month. This opportunity has continuously presented me with fresh challenges, fuelling my passion and commitment to web development. As I look forward, I am excited to dive into the world of React.js, eager to expand my expertise and elevate the dynamic web experiences I can offer. Thank you for taking an interest in my journey!
